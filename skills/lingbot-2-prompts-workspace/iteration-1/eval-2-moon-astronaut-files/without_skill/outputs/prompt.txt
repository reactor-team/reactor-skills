Idle behavior when no input is given: the astronaut stands in place on the regolith, swaying very slightly, boots settled in the dust, sunlight glinting off the gold visor, the camera holding steady behind them. The scene stays calm and stable: same terrain, same lighting, same black sky, ready to respond to player movement at any moment.
