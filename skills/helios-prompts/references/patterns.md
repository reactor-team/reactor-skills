## Pattern 1: Environmental Interaction

Subject experiences an active environment (rain, wind, petals, fireworks).

**Arc:** Calm → environment activates → subject interacts → peak effect → settled state

**Use for:** Cherry blossom picnic, rainy evening, snowfall, windy field

**Example sequence outline (Cherry Blossom Picnic):**

- Start: Woman on blanket under cherry tree, calm, no wind
- Follow-up 1: Gentle breeze starts, first petals drift down
- Follow-up 2: Wind picks up, petals swirl around her, she raises hand
- Follow-up 3: Peak — petals everywhere, caught in hair, covering blanket
- Follow-up 4: Wind calms, petals settle, she brushes one from her cheek

## Pattern 2: Object Reveal/Transformation

Something hidden is revealed or changes state.

**Arc:** Initial state (covered, closed, unlit) → transition begins → reveal moment → reaction → settled enjoyment

**Use for:** Fine dining cloche lift, flower blooming, gift unwrapping, lantern release

**Example sequence outline (Fine Dining Cloche):**

- Start: Elegant woman at restaurant table, covered silver cloche before her
- Follow-up 1: Waiter lifts cloche — aromatic steam billows upward, swirling in candlelight
- Follow-up 2: Steam dissipates revealing the dish, woman leans forward, eyes widen
- Follow-up 3: She picks up fork, brings first bite to mouth, expression of delight
- Follow-up 4: She sets fork down, leans back with satisfied smile, steam fully gone

## Pattern 3: Escalating Intensity

A single element increases in quantity or power.

**Arc:** Calm baseline → first instance → multiple instances → peak intensity → sustained or settling

**Use for:** Fireworks display, lantern festival, gathering storm, city lights turning on

**Example sequence outline (NYC Fireworks):**

- Start: Drone over Manhattan at night, city lights glowing, calm sky
- Follow-up 1: Single firework bursts above skyline, light reflects off buildings
- Follow-up 2: Three more bursts in rapid succession, different colors
- Follow-up 3: Full sky of fireworks, light dancing across every building surface
- Follow-up 4: Grand finale — sustained bursts, smoke drifting through light

---

## Pattern 4: Character Action Sequence

Subject performs a series of related micro-actions.

**Arc:** At rest → preparatory action → main action → result/reaction → settling or celebration

**Use for:** Birthday candles, racing flag wave, cooking, playing instrument

**Example sequence outline (Racing Flag):**

- Start: Official at track edge holding checkered flag, cars approaching
- Follow-up 1: Raises flag above head with both hands, fabric catches wind
- Follow-up 2: Waves flag in wide arc, fabric snaps loudly
- Follow-up 3: Cars roar past, flag still waving, wind from cars ripples clothing
- Follow-up 4: Lowers flag, cars distant now, dust settling on track

## Full Example Walkthrough: Birthday Candles

### Start Prompt (Prompt 0)

> A young boy named Max sits at a wooden dining table in a suburban living room. He wears a bright red party hat with gold stars that sits slightly tilted on his curly brown hair. In front of him sits a large frosted birthday cake with five lit candles, their small flames flickering gently. The background shows a beige couch, family photos on the wall, and soft linen curtains gently swaying by an open window. A few colorful wrapped presents are stacked on the couch beside some balloons. The warm afternoon sunlight streams through the linen curtains, casting a soft glow across the room. The scene captures the festive, happy atmosphere of a child's birthday party. Shot on a 90s VHS Handicam with characteristic grain, slight tracking lines, and warm, slightly oversaturated colors typical of old home movies. Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake.

**Breakdown:**

- Subject: Max, red party hat with gold stars, curly brown hair, at wooden dining table
- Environment: beige couch, family photos, linen curtains, presents, balloons
- Lighting anchor: "warm afternoon sunlight streams through the linen curtains"
- Style: 90s VHS Handicam
- Camera: "Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake."

### Follow-Up 1: "Taking a Breath"

> The young boy named Max remains seated at the wooden dining table in the sunlit suburban living room, the frosted birthday cake still in front of him with five lit candles flickering. He suddenly leans forward slightly, his small face illuminated by the candlelight as he takes a deep breath in preparation to blow. The warm afternoon light continues to filter through the linen curtains, illuminating the beige couch and family photos in the background. Shot on a 90s VHS Handicam with characteristic grain, slight tracking lines, and warm, slightly oversaturated colors typical of old home movies. Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake.

**What changed:** ONE action — leans forward and takes a breath. State: candles still lit, hat still tilted.

### Follow-Up 2: "Make a Wish"

> The young boy named Max sits at the wooden dining table in the suburban living room, leaning forward toward the birthday cake. He now purses his lips and blows forcefully at the five candles. The flames flicker wildly before extinguishing completely, all five candles going out. Thin wisps of white smoke curl upward from the extinguished wicks, drifting through the warm sunlight streaming through the linen curtains. The warm afternoon light continues to filter through the linen curtains. Shot on a 90s VHS Handicam with characteristic grain, slight tracking lines, and warm, slightly oversaturated colors typical of old home movies. Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake.

**What changed:** ONE action — blows out candles. Environmental reaction: smoke curls upward. State: candles now extinguished and smoking.

### Follow-Up 3: "Smile for the Camera"

> The young boy named Max sits at the wooden dining table, the frosted birthday cake with five extinguished, unlit candles before him. He suddenly turns his head to look directly into the camera lens, his eyes widening with excitement as a huge, gap-toothed smile spreads across his face. His bright red party hat sits crooked on his curly brown hair, nearly falling to one side. The warm afternoon sunlight continues to stream through the linen curtains. Shot on a 90s VHS Handicam with characteristic grain, slight tracking lines, and warm, slightly oversaturated colors typical of old home movies. Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake.

**What changed:** ONE action — turns to camera and smiles. Progressive states: candles "extinguished, unlit" (smoke gone), hat "crooked, nearly falling to one side" (was "slightly tilted").

### Follow-Up 4: "Wave Hello"

> The young boy named Max remains at the wooden dining table, still looking toward the camera with his gap-toothed smile, the frosted birthday cake with its five unlit candles in front of him. He now raises his right hand and waves enthusiastically at the camera. His bright red party hat has slipped completely to one side of his curly brown hair. The smoke wisps from the candles have now fully dissipated. The warm afternoon light continues to stream through the linen curtains, casting a soft glow across the room. A faint VHS timestamp reading '05/12/1997' flickers in the lower right corner. Shot on a 90s VHS Handicam with characteristic grain, slight tracking lines, and warm, slightly oversaturated colors typical of old home movies. Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake.

**What changed:** ONE action — raises hand and waves. Progressive states: hat "slipped completely to one side," smoke "fully dissipated," VHS timestamp added as final detail.

## Start Prompt Examples by Category

### People

- "A young boy named Max sits at a wooden dining table in a suburban living room."
- "An elegant woman sits at a pristine white-clothed table in an upscale restaurant."

### Nature

- "A single pink cherry blossom bud sits at the tip of a slender branch."
- "A majestic lion named Leo stands proudly atop a rocky outcrop on the African savanna."

### Aerial/Urban

- "A drone glides smoothly over downtown Manhattan at night."
- "A vintage streetcar rolls down a cobblestone street in San Francisco."

### Lighting Anchor Examples

- "Soft morning light illuminates the scene."
- "The warm afternoon light continues to filter through the linen curtains."
- "A warm halogen glow stretches across the entire city."
- "Candlelight flickers gently, casting dancing shadows across the table."

### Camera Instruction Examples

- "Medium close-up shot focusing on Max and the birthday cake."
- "Extreme close-up macro shot with shallow depth of field, focusing on the flower bud."
- "Wide angle drone shot capturing the expansive cityscape from above."
- "Over-the-shoulder medium shot focusing on the woman and the covered plate."
